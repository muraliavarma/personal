Graduate research project: finger tracking via Microsoft Kinect depth sensors to simulate soccer in 3D.

- Tracked finger positions in 3D space using Kinect IR sensors and OpenNI
- Calculated slope approximations in XY/YZ planes via over-constrained problem solving
- Rendered with OpenGL; built custom depth confidence algorithms to reduce jitter from noisy IR data
