Procedurally generated forests using L-systems and Three.js.

- Trees compete for light and exhibit geotropism (gravity-induced sagging)
- Turtle graphics draw trees from L-system grammar rules
- Randomized seed distribution across iterations; older trees (red) grow tall and eventually die, creating dynamic ecosystem cycles
