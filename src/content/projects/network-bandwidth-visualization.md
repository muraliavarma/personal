Real-time network monitoring tool for live TV feed transmission during the Commonwealth Games.

- Visualized router topology, bandwidth utilization per link bundle, and points of failure
- Tracked primary and backup paths of individual TV feeds across stadium infrastructure
- Enabled operators to identify backup link positions and monitor large-scale networks from a browser
